# 02 — Timeline

All times are local to Bucharest. All teams are on-site.

> **Note for organizers**: exact hours marked `HH:MM` need to be filled in before publishing to participants.

## Saturday

| Time | Event |
|---|---|
| `Sat 10:00` | Challenge opens. Participant repo is pushed. `data/dev.pdf`, `questions/calibration.md`, `questions/midday.md`, `questions/cyberedu.md` all live. Midday CyberEDU channel opens. CyberEDU mini channel opens. |
| `Sun 01:00` | End of day. Each team can see **its own** CyberEDU mini scores. Midday scores remain hidden. No rankings visible. |

## Leaderboard visibility summary

- **Saturday end-of-day**: you see your own CyberEDU mini result. Nobody sees rankings or midday scores.
- **Sunday 10:30**: full leaderboard for everything scored so far (CyberEDU mini + midday).
- **Sunday post-sprint**: leaderboard updates with the final sprint.
- **Sunday post-jury**: final aggregate.

## Appeals window

Scoring appeals must be submitted within **30 minutes** of the leaderboard release that contains the disputed score. See [`04_scoring.md`](04_scoring.md) for the appeal procedure.
